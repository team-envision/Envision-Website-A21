Team Envision is a multidisciplinary technical oriented team of Aaruush that aims to provide solutions to most campus and societal issues. It is a consortium of AI, IoT, Blockchain, and Web developers and designers who work closely to ideate, fabricate and develop products that tackle the above-mentioned problems. Team Envision promotes learning and growth through effective solutions. Our team comprises diligent and meticulous workers, who through their skills have won various accolades for our institution. As a team, we have participated in various hackathons, namely Rajasthan Hackathon, Mozofest Hackathon, Accenture Blockchain, etc and have come out victorious.
